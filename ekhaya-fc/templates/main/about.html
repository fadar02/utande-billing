About Ekhaya FC

Our story, our values, our home.

Our Story

Ekhaya Football Club was founded with a singular vision: to create a football club that represents the heart and soul of its community. The name "Ekhaya" means "home" in Zulu, embodying our belief that football is more than a game — it's a family.

From humble beginnings, we have grown into a competitive force, developing local talent and fostering a culture of excellence both on and off the pitch. Our players are not just athletes; they are ambassadors of discipline, teamwork, and community pride.

Today, Ekhaya FC stands as a testament to what can be achieved when passion meets purpose. We continue to invest in youth development, community engagement, and sporting excellence.
